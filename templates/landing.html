How it works

1

Select your source folder

Point Fetch to the folder containing all your files. It will recursively search through all subfolders to find matches.

2

Choose your reference

Either select a folder with reference files, or paste a list of filenames. Fetch will match based on filename, ignoring extensions.

3

Process and organize

Preview the matches, then copy or move them to your output folder. Made a mistake? Use the one-click undo feature.
